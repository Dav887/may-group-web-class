Labrador Retvier

About Labrador Retvier

The Labrador Retvier.

often abbreviated to Labrador or Lab, is a breed of retriever-gun dog from the United Kingdom that was developed from imported Canadian fishing dogs. The Labrador is one of the most popular dog breeds in a number of countries in the world, particularly in the Western world.

Color

  • Black (a solid black colour)
  • Yellow (considered from creamy white to fox-red)
  • Chocolate (medium to dark brown and originally called Liver)

Health

Healthy Labradors typically live to between 12 and 14 years of age.Labrador pups generally are not brought to the home before they are 8 weeks old.

Apperance

Strong body, energetic, wide skull, chest with wide deep ribs, waist a back wide firm. Labradors are medium in size, the male weighs 29-36 kg, the female - 25-32 kg
